Taiwan Culture Society

The Taiwan Culture Society (Chinese: 台灣文化協會; Pinyin: Táiwān wénhuà xiéhuì) was an organisation created by Taiwanese university students in Japan. They called for increased autonomy and self-determination for the Taiwanese people, greater freedoms, democratic reforms and a separate Taiwanese parliament.
