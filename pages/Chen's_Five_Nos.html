Chen's Five Nos

The Five Nos were a set of five principles Chen Shui-bian swore to adhere to in his inaugural speach. These included:

  • No declaration of independence,
  • No change to the ROC's official name,
  • No referendum on the political status of Taiwan,
  • No reference to "state-to-state relations" in the Constitution.
  • No abolition of the National Unification Council.
